Bio

In September 2023, I graduated with a double Ph.D. in Artificial Intelligence from Sapienza University in Rome (Italy) and York University in Toronto (Canada), under the co-supervision of Giuseppe De Giacomo and Yves Lespérance. My Ph.D. dissertation focused on Artificial Intelligence, Formal Methods, and Business Process Management and Automation. For the latest 3 years of my Ph.D. I worked in the WhiteMech research group.

In October 2018, I completed my M.Sc. in Engineering in Computer Science at Sapienza University working with Giuseppe De Giacomo. Prior to that, I completed my B.Sc. in Industrial Engineering at Campus Bio-Medico University working with Luca Vollero.
